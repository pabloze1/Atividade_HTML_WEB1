Coluna

Corsair is a branch of the Explorer Pirate class. Upon reaching Level 30, an Explorer Pirate can become a Corsair by choosing to become a Gunslinger after completing the 2nd Job Advancement at Level 30. Their second job is Gunslinger, their third job is Outlaw (Valkyrie in other versions), and their fourth job is Corsair (Captain in other versions).

Coluna

Buccaneer is a branch of the Explorer Pirate class. Upon reaching Level 30, an Explorer Pirate can become a Buccaneer by choosing to become a Brawler (Infighter in other versions) after completing the 2nd Job Advancement at Level 30. Their second job is Brawler / Infighter, their third job is Outlaw (Buccaneer in other versions), and their fourth job is Buccaneer (Viper in other versions).
